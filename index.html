About

Learning & Development professional, specialising in Digital Learning

10+ years designing and delivering training — including 16 years with Emirates Airlines and a year building the e-learning platform for a 26,000-employee healthcare organisation. I work end-to-end: LMS administration and SCORM packaging, instructional design, video production, and the graphic design and interactive tools that make training worth sitting through.

This site is a working sample of that range — touchscreen training apps built from scratch, a curated reel of produced videos, the service manuals and presentations behind them, and the photography from the programmes themselves.
